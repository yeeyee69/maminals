It is known that leaves change color, proof...
look outside for a year, youll see they change colors, but why.

It all has to do with photosynthisis, and here is how this process works.
"Leaves are nature's food factories. Plants take water from the ground through
their roots. They take a gas called carbon dioxide from the air. Plants use
sunlight to turn water and carbon dioxide into oxygen and glucose. Oxygen is a
gas in the air that we need to breathe. Glucose is a kind of sugar. Plants use
glucose as food for energy and as a building block for growing."
